How NFT Breath me was created

We were driving home from Georgia to Krasnodar, before this photo was taken we spent the whole day on the road and the night waiting for the road to be cleared of an avalanche.

It was Olga Naida's most grueling trip, but it will be remembered for a lifetime. She had to spend a total of 30 hours in traffic jams before the border, not to mention another 15 hours on the road. After climbing through the mountain tunnels and serpentine steep serpentines, she wanted very much to get out and breathe with her lungs the aroma of the plain.

That's why photo "Breath me" is used as a cover for articles about tranquility, getting out of depression, or inspiration.
